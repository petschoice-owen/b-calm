How it works

What is CBD Oil?

Better known as CBD, Cannabidoil is a popular natural remedy. It is used for many common ailments, such as pain management, to help manage anxiety and to promote general well-being. CBD oil is a chemical compound found in the cannabis plant, Cannabis sativa.

How does B-Calm work?

B-Calm uses CBD isolate oil extracted from the flowers, leaves and stems of cannabis plants. When it is sprayed onto pet bedding it is absorbed through the skin and affects the neurological pathways within the pet's brain. This has a calming effect and aids relaxation. Unlike full-spectrum CBD products, CBD isolate does not contain any THC.

Is it safe?

Yes. As the product does not contain THC, it is non-intoxicating, meaning that it does not create a 'high' giving you the confidence that it is not only safe, but effective.

Instructions for Use

Apply spray directly to your pet's bedding and/or immediate surroundings

If spraying onto fabrics, try a small inconspicuous area first

If your pet continues to show signs of stress, please consult your vet.

Do not apply directly to your pet

Do not administer orally

Do not apply to treated leather objects, such as sofas.
